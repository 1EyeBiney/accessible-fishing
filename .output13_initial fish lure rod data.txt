Phase 2 Proposal — Fish / Lure / Rod Schemas
Design Axioms (binding all three schemas)
**Identity / Traits / State partition** (mirrors D-037 tile schema). Rods and Lures are pure traits (no mutable state beyond durability). Fish *species* is traits; an *instance* fish carries state.
**All cross-entity math runs through normalized [0..1] match scores.** No raw stat comparisons in consumers — every interaction is a `matchScore(a, b) → [0..1]` function owned by the consumer module.
**No schema field exists without a named consumer.** If `strikeModel.js` / `hookset.js` / `tensionModel.js` / `castResolver.js` doesn't read it, it doesn't ship.
**Procedural-audio compatibility (D-028)**: every entity exposes an `audio` block of synth-friendly scalars (no asset paths).

Key Equations (proposed; all bounded [0..1] unless noted)
Cast distance (castResolver.js):
$$\text{dist} = \text{basePwr}(\text{rod.pwrIndex}) \cdot f(\text{lure.weightOz}, \text{rod.lureWeightRangeOz}) \cdot \text{tapPower}$$
where $f$ is a parabola peaking at sweet, dropping off at the range edges. Out-of-range lures incur an accuracy penalty (variance on landing tile) — they still cast.
Strike probability (strikeModel.js):
$$P_{\text{strike}} = P_{\text{base}}(\text{species,tile}) \cdot M_{\text{lure↔fish}} \cdot M_{\text{lure↔tile}} \cdot S(\text{spook}) \cdot R(\text{pressure}) \cdot D(\text{diurnal}) \cdot I(\text{lureMemory})$$
$M_{\text{lure↔fish}} = \langle \text{lure.presentationProfile}, \text{fish.presentationPreferences}\rangle \cdot \text{lure.speciesAffinity}[\text{species}]$
$M_{\text{lure↔tile}}$ = depth band overlap × bottom affinity × cover affinity × tag affinity
$S(s) = 1 - 0.18 s$ (per-level penalty; spook 5 ⇒ 10% of base)
$R(p) = 1 - (\text{PRESSURE\_STRIKE\_PENALTY} \cdot p / \text{MAX\_PRESSURE})$ (D-039 — at p=5, multiplier = 0.4)
$I(\text{mem})$ shrinks if same lure id was just refused by this instance; magnitude scaled by `intelligence`
Hookset window width (hookset.js, supersedes raw 750ms baseline of D-033 — shrink-only still holds because product ≤ 750ms when species mouthHardness > rod.hooksetSensitivity):
$$w_{\text{ms}} = 750 \cdot \text{rod.hooksetSensitivity} \cdot (1 - 0.5 \cdot \text{fish.mouthHardness})$$
Floor at 200ms. Rod with FAST action (1.15) on Bass (mouth 0.6): $750 \cdot 1.15 \cdot 0.70 = 603 \text{ms}$.
Tension delta per 60ms tick (tensionModel.js):
$$\Delta T = \big[ F_{\text{fish}}(\text{phase, spike}) \cdot m_{\text{phase}} - C(\text{rod}) \cdot \text{playerInput}(t) - \text{drag} \big] \cdot \Delta t$$
$F_{\text{fish}}$ samples `pullForceCurve` with phase, intermittent spikes per `spikeIntervalMs`
Snap when $T \geq 1.0$ → `LINE_SNAPPED` (also damages rod by `damagePerSnap`)
Slack when $T \leq 0.0$ for ≥`SLACK_GRACE_MS` × (1 − `hookRetention` × 0.5) → `HOOK_SHAKEN`
Phase transition: `instance.stamina ≤ 0` → `TIRED`; reel-in distance → `FISH_LANDED`
Phase-dependent idle decay (D-031): when both Spacebar and ArrowDown are held (mutex violation) OR neither is held, $\Delta T$ uses tensionMultipliers[phase] only — Running drifts up toward RUNNING_BASE, Tired drifts down toward TIRED_BASE.
5. Hazards Implied by These Schemas (proposed additions)
**H-015 Stat-bloat creep** — every new field invites design drift. Lock the schemas at v1.7 and require a `D-` entry to add a field.
**H-016 Affinity-table sparsity** — empty enum keys in `bottomAffinity`/`coverAffinity` must default explicitly (proposal: `0.0` if absent), not silently zero through undefined.
**H-017 Lure-memory unbounded growth** — `instance.lureMemory[]` must be capped (proposal: last 3 lures) or it leaks across long fights.

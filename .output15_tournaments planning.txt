3. Career Progression & Hub State
**Player Progression**: Player's `profile` and `hub` state tracks:
- `unlockedTournaments: [tourneyId, ...]`
- `currentMoney`
- `ownedGear: [itemId, ...]`
- `activeRodId`, `activeLureId`, etc.
- `calendarDay` (for AI arms race and event unlocks)
**Tournament Unlocking**: Tournaments are unlocked by winning/loss, calendar progression, or explicit purchases.
**Entry Fees**: Deducted from `hub.money` on entry. If insufficient, entry is blocked.
**Prize Distribution**: On tournament end, `tournament/payout.js` awards prizes to player based on final standing. AI bots do NOT receive or spend money—only their `aiGearTier` is advanced as the calendar progresses.
4. Arms Race AI Model
**AI Gear Tier**: Each tournament event specifies an `aiGearTier` (1–5). All AI bots in that event use this tier, regardless of prior results.
**Calendar Progression**: As the player advances the in-game calendar (by entering tournaments or via explicit time skips), future tournaments increase their `aiGearTier` to simulate the arms race.
**No AI Wallets**: AI bots do not have persistent money, inventory, or progression. Their challenge is set by the event's `aiGearTier`.
5. Integration Points
**hub/**: Manages player inventory, money, unlocked tournaments, and calendar. Tournament entry checks and prize claims are routed through hub state.
**tournament/payout.js**: Reads the event's `prizes` block and the player's final standing to award gear or money. No AI payout logic required.
**tournament/leaderboard.js**: Ranks player and AI bots by event win condition (heaviest bag, biggest fish, etc.).
**ai/**: Each bot is instantiated with the event's `aiGearTier` and a personality profile (to be modeled in Phase 3).
6. Architectural Benefits
**Replayability**: Static lakes + dynamic tournaments + arms race AI = infinite replay without simulating AI economies.
**Performance**: No need to track AI wallets, inventories, or persistent state. All AI challenge is event-driven.
**Balance**: Player always has a clear goal: outpace the AI arms race by winning, earning, and upgrading before the next tier.
7. Example Tournament Flow
Player enters "Weekend Beginner's Bash" (tier 1, aiGearTier 1, entryFee 0).
On win, player receives new rod (prize), unlocks next tournament.
Calendar advances; next event has aiGearTier 2.
Player must upgrade gear to keep pace or risk being outclassed.
